Examples/Instructions

Build a pyramid-shaped tower, as an array/list of strings, given a positive integer number of floors. A tower block is represented with "*" character.

3 floors -> [ " * ", " *** ", "*****" ]

6 floors -> [ " * ", " *** ", " ***** ", " ******* ", " ********* ", "***********" ]
